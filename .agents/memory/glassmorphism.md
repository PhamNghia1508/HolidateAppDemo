---
name: GatherGo Glassmorphism System
description: Design tokens for glassmorphism cards, backgrounds, and gradient CTAs used across all 10 screens.
---

**Glass Card Formula:**
- `backdrop-blur-md` (12-16px blur)
- `bg-white/60` to `bg-white/70` (never bg-white/10 with blur — too dark)
- `border border-white/50` to `border-white/60`
- `rounded-[20px]` or `rounded-[16px]`
- Subtle shadow: `shadow-lg shadow-sage/10`

**Background:**
- Base: `linear-gradient(180deg, #f3eee8 0%, #e8e0d6 100%)`
- Cream base: `#f3eee8`

**Gradient CTA:**
- `linear-gradient(135deg, #e76f51, #f4a261)`
- Text: white, bold

**Color Palette:**
- Sage: `#4a7c59` — primary actions, icons
- Coral: `#e76f51` — accent, highlights
- Yellow: `#e9c46a` — secondary accent
- Ink: `#2a2a2a` — primary text
